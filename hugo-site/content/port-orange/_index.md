## Signs You Have a Rodent Problem

You might not see a mouse or rat right away. But the clues are hard to miss once you know what to look for.

- **Droppings** near food storage, along baseboards, or under sinks
- **Gnaw marks** on wood, plastic pipes, or electrical wiring
- **Scratching or scurrying sounds** inside walls or ceilings at night
- **Shredded nesting material** made from paper, insulation, or fabric
- **Grease smears** along baseboards where rodents travel repeatedly
